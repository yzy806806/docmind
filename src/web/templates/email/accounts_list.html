How It Works

  1. Configure an IMAP account (host, credentials, folder to monitor).
  2. The background worker polls for unseen emails at the configured interval.
  3. Email body and attachments are extracted and saved as searchable documents.
  4. Processed emails are marked as read (mark-to-read only — move/delete deferred).
  5. Filter documents by source_type = email on the Documents page.
